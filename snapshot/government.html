GOVERNMENT

More than the virus, the failure of the government to address major social issues like poverty, housing, food security, and gender based violence is what is putting us in danger now and long-term. Those of us organizing this rent strike believe that this is just how governments function, and that itâ€™s our job as regular people to come together to fulfil our own needs rather than have representatives pretend to do things in our interest.

However, for those of you who want to engage government further, we recommend that you demand the following protect our health and ensure that everyone has shelter.

All levels of government must take measures to:

  • Cancel rent (no paying back later)
  • Suspend (not defer) mortgages and foreclosures
  • Reduce or eliminate utilities bills
  • Stop evictions of land occupations as we have seen in eThekwini and Khayelitsha
  • Deepen its debt relief commitments further.
